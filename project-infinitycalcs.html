InfinityCalcs

InfinityCalcs is a YouTube channel that I created in high school showing students how to write programs on their graphing calculator as well as how to activate some features such brightness and archiving.

Over the years, the channel has amassed a +600,000 views and has helped students around the world. Some of the most visited countries include the United States, Canada, Netherlands, Portugal & Belgium.

Given the popularity, I started to build a website to compliment the YouTube channel which can be found here.
