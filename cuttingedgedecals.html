Cutting Edge Decals

Cutting Edge Decals is a venture started by three UCLA students (Suhani Ashok, Kyle Wilcots, and Ronak Patel) that designs and creates customized decals primarily for UCLA student organizations and clubs. We noticed that creating customized decals online required large minimum orders and that they did not truly allow you to design anything you wanted. Additionally, you would be hit with a shipping and handling fee that increased your costs. As such, we decided to create decals of UCLA student organization and club names in the exact order amount they specified and hand-deliver the order to them. This drastically reduced the per decal cost for organizations and clubs who would have otherwise purchased them online.

For the Spring Quarter of 2015, Cutting Edge Decals received orders from 5 organizations and 20 individuals resulting in revenues of $125. We plan to further research the idea of partnering with print shops and having our decals cut using lasers, which will allow for greater precision and more standardized decals.
