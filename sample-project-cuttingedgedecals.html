Cutting Edge Decals

Cutting Edge Decals was a venture started by three UCLA students (Suhani Ashok, Kyle Wilcots, and Ronak Patel) that designed and created customized decals for UCLA student organizations and clubs. We noticed that creating customized decals online required large minimum orders and they did not truly allow you to create custom designs. Additionally, a shipping and handling fee would be incurred for most online sites. We decided to handmake decals for UCLA student organizations and clubs wanting to provide their members swag at affordable prices. For small clubs with less than 20 members who could not meet online order minimums, we were able to come in and offer our service.

For the Spring Quarter of 2015, Cutting Edge Decals received orders from 5 organizations and 20 individuals resulting in revenues of $125. We plan to further research the idea of partnering with local print shops and having our decals laser cut to ensure precision and more standardized decals.
